Our Story

Built on Trust, Driven by Quality

ApexStore was founded in 2020 by a group of tech enthusiasts and retail veterans who believed the e-commerce experience was broken. Too many marketplaces. Too little curation. Zero accountability.

We hand-pick every product category, test quality standards ourselves, and stand behind every item we sell. From day one, our return policy has been straightforward: if you're not happy, we'll make it right.

Today we serve customers in 40+ countries, ship over 5,000 orders a month, and maintain a 4.9-star average rating — because we refuse to list anything we wouldn't buy ourselves.
